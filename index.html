Membership

InterNACHI® offers everything you need to run a succesful home inspection company, whether you are a start-up or a succesful multi-inspector firm.

2

Education

InterNACHI® School is the only home inspector school accredited by the Accrediting Council for Continuing Education & Training (ACCET), a national accrediting agency of the U.S. Department of Education. As a member, you have free and unlimited access to all of InterNACHI® School’s online courses, including its Certified Professional Inspector (CPI)® certification program.

3

House of Horrors

The House of Horrors® is a full-size house with thousands of defects purposely built in. Get hands-on at this first-of-its-kind training facility at locations around the U.S.

4

Community

Join the largest community, online and offline, in the home inspection industry. InterNACHI® hosts online forums, annual conventions, nationwide chapter meetings, and live online events.

5

Marketing

InterNACHI® offers free professional design services for its members’ inspection companies. You pay only for the cost of printing and shipping.

6

Specialty Programs

Take advantage of InterNACHI’s specialty programs to add value to your inspection services.

7

Media

Join us for live webinars, training videos, and podcasts with top industry professionals.
